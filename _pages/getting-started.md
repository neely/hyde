This page is part of the Hyde template. Replace or delete it once your blog is set up — your readers don't need to see your own setup instructions.

### Writing posts

Open [`/compose.html`](/compose.html) on this site from your phone or computer. Fill in the title, date, and tags, write the body in Markdown, and hit publish. It commits straight to this repository.

See the full setup walkthrough in this repository's `README.md` for forking, GitHub Pages, and Personal Access Token instructions.

### Categories

You can categorize content using `categories` in front matter:

```md
categories: [PHP, Laravel]
```

Categorized content is listed at `/categories/`.

### RSS

The generated RSS feed of your blog is at `/feed`.

### Sitemap

The generated sitemap of your blog is at `/sitemap.xml`.
